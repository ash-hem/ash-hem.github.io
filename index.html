Ash Hem

Hi I'm Ash! I am a lived experience peer supporter, supervisor, educator and facilitator. I approach my work from a collaborative, co-creative and social justice perspective, bringing my own experiences of mental health, disability, neurodivergence, queerness, cultural background and lived experience work into the space.

I have a work background in peer work, training and education, facilitation, digital mental health, creative-based support and broader lived experience work, across mental health, complex trauma, queer, disability, trauma, migrant/refugee and youth spaces.

I work from a creative, neuro-affirming perspective where we get clear on what we want and need from the space from the start and throughout our work. I encourage and welcome authentic expression when working with people, doing what we need to get the most out of working together

I currently provide peer support, peer supervision, facilitation and consulting. Get in touch with me via this form or email me at ash.hem@outlook.com
